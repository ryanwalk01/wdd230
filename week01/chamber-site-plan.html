Ryan Walker's Parkersburg Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Parkersburg Chamber of Commerce. Parkersburg is my home town, which is where the Chamber will operate.

Site Purpose

The Parkersburg Chamber of Commerce website will allow companies in the Parkersburg area to voice their thoughts and support one another.

They will have the ability to advertise their business and gain funding which will allow their business to grow.

Scenarios

  1. What are some local businesses I can support?
  2. What are the hours for the local cafe?
  3. What business has been around the longest?
